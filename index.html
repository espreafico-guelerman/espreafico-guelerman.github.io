About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Mathematics at the Institut de Mathématiques de Jussieu – Paris Rive Gauche (IMJ-PRG), Sorbonne Université, Paris, working within the Équipe Analyse Algébrique under the supervision of Professor Penka Georgieva.

My research interests center around enumerative aspects of Algebraic Geometry, Hodge theory, and their relations with Physics. Recently, I've been working in the field of A¹-enumerative geometry, aiming to understand how physical invariants can be realized in this context, as well as exploring more general enumerative problems.

Before joining Sorbonne Université, I completed my PhD in February 2024 at the Instituto Nacional de Matemática Pura e Aplicada (IMPA), Rio de Janeiro, under the supervision of Professor Hossein Movasati and co-supervision of Professor Johannes Walcher from the University of Heidelberg.
